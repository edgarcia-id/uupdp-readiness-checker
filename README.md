### 🚨 The Compliance Challenge in Indonesia
With the enforcement of **UU PDP No. 27/2022**, companies operating in Indonesia face strict regulatory requirements regarding personal data handling. Non-compliance can result in severe administrative sanctions, including fines up to **2% of the company's annual global revenue**.

Many organizations, IT Managers, and newly appointed Data Protection Officers (DPO) struggle to baseline their current IT infrastructure and operational SOPs against the new legal standards.

### 💡 The Solution: Automated Gap Analysis
This open-source assessment tool acts as an industry-standard baseline for IT Consultants and internal auditors. It evaluates 12 critical control points across 5 core pillars:

1.  **Governance & Accountability:** Lawful basis, Explicit Consent, and DPO appointment.
2.  **Data Subject Rights:** Right to Access, Update, and Erasure (Right to be Forgotten).
3.  **IT Security & Processing:** Data Minimization, Encryption (At Rest & In Transit), DPIA (Data Protection Impact Assessment), and IAM.
4.  **Vendor Management:** DPA (Data Processing Agreements) and Cross-Border Data Transfers.
5.  **Incident Response:** 3x24 Hours Data Breach Notification protocol.

### 🚀 Key Features
* **Automated Scoring:** Instantly calculates your organization's compliance readiness percentage.
* **Executive PDF Report:** Generates a professional Audit & Gap Analysis report ready for board-level presentation.
* **100% Privacy Focused (Client-Side):** This tool runs entirely in your browser. No company data, inputs, or scores are sent to any server or database.
