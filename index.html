UU PDP Readiness Audit

Comprehensive Self-Assessment for Indonesia Law No. 27/2022

Standardized by Ed Garcia (NusaIT)
Part 1: Governance & Accountability

1.1 Lawful Basis & Explicit Consent

Do you have a clear, documented lawful basis (e.g., explicit consent, contractual necessity) before collecting any personal data?

1.2 Privacy Notice Transparency

Is there a clear Privacy Policy available to data subjects detailing what data is collected, why, and how long it is stored?

1.3 Data Protection Officer (DPO)

Has the organization formally appointed a Data Protection Officer (Pejabat/Petugas Pelindungan Data Pribadi)?
Part 2: Data Subject Rights (Hak Subjek Data)

2.1 Right to Access & Update

Do you have a mechanism (UI/SOP) allowing users to request access to or correction of their personal data?

2.2 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)

Can you completely purge a user's data from active databases and backups upon their legitimate request?
Part 3: IT Security & Processing Principles

3.1 Data Minimization & Retention

Do you automatically destroy or anonymize personal data when the retention period (or legal requirement) expires?

3.2 Encryption in Transit & At Rest

Are sensitive personal data (passwords, medical, financial, KTP) encrypted within your databases and via HTTPS/TLS?

3.3 Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA)

Do you conduct a DPIA before launching new applications or features that process high-risk personal data?

3.4 Access Control (Principle of Least Privilege)

Is access to production databases containing personal data strictly limited, logged, and periodically reviewed?
Part 4: Third-Party & Vendor Management

4.1 Data Processing Agreements (DPA)

Do you have legal contracts (DPA) with third-party vendors (Cloud, SaaS, Marketing agencies) that process data on your behalf?

4.2 Cross-Border Data Transfer

If your servers/vendors are located outside Indonesia, do you ensure the destination country has equal or higher data protection standards?
Part 5: Incident Response (Breach Notification)

5.1 72-Hour Breach Notification Plan

Do you have an Incident Response Plan to notify the Authority and affected Data Subjects within 3x24 hours of discovering a leak?
